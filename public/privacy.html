Privacy Policy

This policy explains how UBI Central handles account data, including data received from Google and GitHub sign-in.

What we collect

- Email address and name when you sign up locally, with Google Sign-In, or with GitHub.

- Passwords for local accounts are stored only as bcrypt password hashes.

- Authentication tokens (JWT access and refresh tokens) are generated to keep you signed in.

How we use data

- To create and authenticate your account, and to keep your session active via access/refresh tokens.

- To provide the features you choose (login, profile, logout). We do not use your data for advertising or resale.

Google API Services compliance

- Google data requested: basic profile and email via Google Sign-In (no other Google scopes).

- Use: to create or sign you in and display your profile. No secondary uses, no sale, and no sharing with third parties beyond what is needed to operate authentication.

- Storage: email and name are stored in our user record; refresh tokens issued by this app are stored to manage sessions. Google OAuth tokens are not reused beyond sign-in.

- Access limits: we request only the minimum scopes and do not permit human access except as required by law, security, or debugging with your consent.

- You can revoke Google access anytime via your Google Account permissions or by contacting us.

For more detail, review the Google API Services User Data Policy.

GitHub sign-in

- GitHub data requested: email and basic profile (via user:email scope).

- Use: to create or sign you in and display your profile. No other processing, no advertising, no resale.

- You can revoke GitHub access in your GitHub account settings.

Sharing

- We do not sell or rent personal data.

- We may share data only to comply with law, enforce terms, or protect users or the service.

Security

- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt.

- Tokens are signed with app secrets; refresh tokens rotate on use.

- Data is transmitted over HTTPS in production. Use HTTPS when deploying.

Data retention and deletion

- User records persist while the account exists.

- You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Children

- The service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Contact

For questions or requests (access, correction, deletion, or revocation of OAuth consent), email: privacy@example.com.
